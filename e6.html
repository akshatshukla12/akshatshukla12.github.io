KRITANSH
ABOUT THE EVENT

With the earth continuously puzzling geologists and mining engineers with its diverse and assorted physiognomy, the need of amendment and revolutionary exploration technique arises. KRITANSH is the quiz competition which will contain some mind-boggling and interesting questions from the field of Mining, Geology, Processing and any other relevant topics related to mining field. The quiz intends to boost the enthusiasm and awareness among the participants. It also aims at providing the students an exposure to the on-going problems and advancement in the field of mining. KRITANSH is being organized with a vision to crisscross the dexterity and the proficiency to analyse real world problems of the participants. With participation from various colleges and a sprinkle of brainstorming question, Geo-Mining Quiz promises to entertain the crowd.

ELIGIBILTY :-
The participant students must be currently pursuing education (undergraduate or postgraduate IN MINING) in a recognized institute.

STRUCTURE :-
1. The competition will be organized in two phases.
2. The first round will have a written examination related to Geology & Mining from which FIVE teams will be selected for the second round.
3. The second round will consist of both theoretical as well as practical rounds like rock identification including audio visual rounds also.

RULES :-
1. Only teams who have registered online would be able to participate.
2. Students from different colleges can form a single team.
3. Each team can contain a maximum of THREE (3) participants.
4. The decision of the judges would be final and binding.
5. Only Top three teams will get certificate of merit. However other teams would get a certificate of participation.
