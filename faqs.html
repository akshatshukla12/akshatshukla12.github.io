FAQs

Q. HOW TO REACH THE NIT CAMPUS ON ARRIVAL AT ROURKELA ?

By train, one may arrive at the ROURKELA station and then hire a auto-rickshaw from station to NIT Rourkela. The auto fare should be about Rs. 120. By Bus,get off at the SECTOR-2 bus stand and hire an auto-rickshaw from the bus stand to NIT Rourkela,at an estimated amount of Rs.70.


Q. WHAT ABOUT ACCOMODATION AFTER REACHING THE CAMPUS?

There will be absolutely no problem in accommodation once you reach us. All the participants will be provided some dormitory sort of arrangements. Special accommodation for girls will be provided . Accommodation will be provided from (24th March, 2017) to (26th March, 2017). Kindly try to be with this scheme. However, if you have any ineluctable problem, don't hesitate to contact the team members and you will definitely be taken care of. Don't forget to bring your college identity card. You will be provided with mattresses, pillows and blankets.


Q. IS THERE ANY REGISTRATION FEE?

There is no fee for online registration and participation in online events. Students coming to NIT Rourkela have to pay a registration fee of Rs 350.


Q. WHERE DO I EAT?

There are a lot of eating corners in the campus such as Hex Restaurant, Jozz Restaurant, Calorie and Amul. You can try out NIT Rourkela's night culture by having some fast food at Night Canteens in the mid night also. There are also a few restaurants outside the campus as well. You can get food in these restaurants at a nominal price, apart from this MINARE will also provide lunch as per schedule.


Q. WHAT FACILITIES ARE AVAILABLE IN THE CAMPUS?

Q. WHEN WILL I GET MY PRIZES AND CERTIFICATES?

The participants will get their certificates on the last day of MINARE during the Prize distribution ceremony. Cash Prizes will be sent directly in to their bank accounts.


Q. HOW DO I CHECK OUT?

For checking out, you need to inform our team. Volunteers will be present at various halls who will collect the blankets, mattresses etc. issued against your name.
