MINARE 2K20

The annual Geo MIning Fest...

MINARE is the annual geo-mining fest of the Department of Mining Engineering. MINARE 2020 is the 5th edition of this extravagant conclave. MINARE is organised with the objective of providing the society of mining engineers in the making of a deeper insight into the subject and its impact on the industry, India being a major reservoir of natural resources like coal and petroleum. Various technical events, workshops, guest lectures, fun events, case study presentation solutions and paper presentation are organised during the course of this mega event. The mining extravaganza not only has a series of competent exhibition of experience and knowledge but also has a pinch of cultural fervour to welcome students from across the length and breadth of the country.
The fest is organised by the Mining Engineering Society with attendees from various institutes of repute who are eager to deepen their knowledge on cutting edge technologies. We are keen at surfacing professionals together for both education and networking.
Like the phoenix, we rise from the ashes and we are planning to take our previous version which witnessed a participation of 200+ students to greater heights. This hugely anticipated edition is set to target 300+ students among the elite institutions of India, which includes IITs, NITs and reputed technical institutes of the country. This fest is limited to students only, no industry/company can participate in any technical events, however they may be allowed to hold exhibitions in our premises.

NIT ROURKELA

National Institute of Technology (NIT) Rourkela, established in 1961 as Regional Engineering College (REC) Rourkela, has come a long way and attained exponential growth since then. Spanning over 648 acres in the land of Rourkela, the industrial hub of Odisha, it is ranked 2nd among NITs in NIRF 2019. The Institute offers 9 different degrees in 20 diverse streams with 500 faculties. The campus has 11 Halls of residences housing over 6000 students. Besides the departmental buildings, the campus also has a central library, a 800 seated auditorium, guest houses, banks and ATMs, post office, health centre, shopping centre, canteens, a central gym, swimming pool, a football-hockey stadium and a cricket ground. NITR has been placed in 16th position in NIRF Engineering 2019, 121st position in QS BRICS 2019, 29th position in QS India University 2020 and within 801-1000 rank in Times World University rankings. Apart from academics, The Institute is also known for its vibrant socio-cultural activities which include Nitrutsav, Innovision, Cosmopolitan festivals etc.

With the vision of having national level students, the Department of Mining Engineering started MINARE in 2014 to make a typical stage for all the budding Mining Engineers. The past versions were loaded with psyche beating difficulties, guest lectures, workshops, technical events and prizes that went for the extension of musings into the unexplored domains of the field.
